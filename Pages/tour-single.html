Enchanting Himalayan Adventure

Tour Overview

Experience the breathtaking beauty of the Himalayas like never before. Our "Enchanting
Himalayan Adventure" tour is a 12-day journey that takes you through some of the most mesmerizing
landscapes and cultural treasures of the region. From the lush valleys to the towering peaks,
this tour promises an unforgettable adventure.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu

  • Arrival in Kathmandu, Nepal
  • Welcome dinner and tour briefing
  • Accommodation: Boutique hotel in Kathmandu

Day 2-4: Annapurna Trek

  • Hike through picturesque Annapurna trails
  • Visit traditional villages and meet the locals
  • Accommodation: Tea houses along the trek route

Day 5-7: Bhutan Exploration

  • Fly to Paro, Bhutan
  • Visit Tiger's Nest Monastery and other cultural sites
  • Experience Bhutanese culture and traditions
  • Accommodation: Bhutanese-style lodges

Day 8-10: River Rafting

  • Rafting adventure in the Bhote Koshi River
  • Thrilling rapids and stunning river landscapes
  • Campfire evenings by the river
  • Accommodation: Riverside campsites

Day 11-12: Return to Kathmandu

  • Return to Kathmandu
  • Free time for shopping and exploration
  • Farewell dinner in Kathmandu
  • Accommodation: Boutique hotel in Kathmandu
